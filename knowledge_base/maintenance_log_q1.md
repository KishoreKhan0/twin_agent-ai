# Maintenance Log Q1

## January Inspection

Routine inspection found no critical issues. Belt tension was adjusted and motor
mounting bolts were checked.

## February Vibration Event

A short vibration warning occurred during high-load production. Inspection found
minor belt tracking deviation. Belt alignment was corrected and vibration
returned to normal.

## March Bearing Observation

Technicians noted slightly increased bearing noise during a routine inspection.
Lubrication was refreshed. The note recommended monitoring vibration and
temperature together during future production runs.

## Lessons Learned

Combined vibration rise and temperature rise should be treated more seriously
than isolated minor deviations. Maintenance staff should compare current events
against previous bearing observations.
