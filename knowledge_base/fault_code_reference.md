# Fault Code Reference

## E-101: Thermal Warning

E-101 indicates motor temperature has exceeded the warning threshold. Check load,
ambient temperature, motor ventilation, and cooling airflow.

## E-102: Thermal Critical

E-102 indicates motor temperature has exceeded the critical operating threshold.
Reduce load and inspect the cooling path before returning to full production.

## E-201: Vibration Warning

E-201 indicates sustained vibration above the warning threshold. Inspect bearing
housing, belt alignment, pulley balance, fasteners, and mounting condition.

## E-202: Vibration Critical

E-202 indicates high vibration that may be caused by bearing wear, misalignment,
pulley imbalance, or mechanical looseness. Reduce load and inspect the machine.

## E-301: Current Overload

E-301 indicates abnormal motor current. Check for excessive load, drive issues,
blocked rollers, or mechanical resistance.

## E-401: Throughput Drop

E-401 indicates that throughput dropped below the expected range. Check belt
speed, belt tracking, material flow, and possible obstruction.
